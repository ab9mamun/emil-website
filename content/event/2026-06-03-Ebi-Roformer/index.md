title: "ROFORMER: ENHANCED TRANSFORMER WITH ROTARY POSITION EMBEDDING"

summary: "This paper introduces RoFormer, an enhanced Transformer architecture that uses Rotary Position Embedding (RoPE) to better encode positional information in self-attention. Unlike traditional positional embeddings that add position vectors to token representations, RoPE applies rotation matrices to queries and keys, allowing the model to naturally capture relative position relationships between tokens. This approach provides useful properties for long-sequence modeling, including flexible sequence length handling, distance-aware attention decay, and compatibility with linear attention mechanisms. Experiments on machine translation, language modeling, GLUE tasks, Performer-based models, and Chinese long-text benchmarks show that RoFormer often converges faster and performs competitively or better than baseline Transformer models, especially on long-text tasks."
