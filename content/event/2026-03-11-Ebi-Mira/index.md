title: "MIRA: Medical Time Series Foundation Model for Real-World Health Data"

summary: "This paper introduces MIRA, a medical time series foundation model designed to forecast real-world health data that are often irregularly sampled, heterogeneous, and incomplete. MIRA combines Continuous-Time Rotary Positional Encoding to model variable time intervals, a frequency-specific Mixture-of-Experts layer to capture different temporal patterns, and a Neural ODE-based extrapolation block to predict future patient states at arbitrary timestamps. Pretrained on more than 454 billion medical time points from diverse public datasets, including ICU records, ECG, sleep, and pediatric waveform data, MIRA achieves strong zero-shot forecasting performance across multiple clinical benchmarks. The study shows that MIRA reduces forecasting errors compared with existing time series foundation models and provides a scalable framework for robust medical time series modeling in real-world healthcare settings."
