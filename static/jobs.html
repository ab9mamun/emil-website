Embedded Machine Intelligence Lab - Available GSA Position

iOS and Android Mobile Application Development and Maintenance

Posted: May 16, 2025

Overview

We are looking for an experienced mobile application developer proficient in both iOS and Android to modify, debug, test, improve, and implement new features for mobile applications designed for data collection, secure transfer, and storage on a remote server.

A key role of the developer will be to provide development and maintenance support for our already developed data collection platforms, which include mobile applications (Android and iOS), a data collection server, and server-side applications.

Developer Requirements

Experience:

  • At least 2 years of experience in developing iOS and Android applications.
  • Proven track record of sending and receiving data to and from servers in iOS and Android applications.
  • Experience with publishing applications to the Apple App Store.
  • Experience with version control systems (Git).

Technical Skills:

  • Proficiency in Swift, Flutter, XCode (latest version), Django framework, and HTTP/HTTPS APIs.
  • Experience with data persistence and management.
  • Familiarity with UI/UX design guidelines for iOS and Android.
  • Experience with the GitHub version control system.

Additional Requirements:

  • The developer must be proactive, responsive, and able to complete tasks responsibly.
  • The developer must create proper documentation of the project as they make progress.

Tools

Must own a MacBook and iPhone for development and testing.

Timeline

Weekly email updates and meetings are required to track progress and ensure alignment with project goals. More frequent updates and/or meetings can be expected near deadlines.
